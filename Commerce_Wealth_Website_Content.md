### Our Story

**Section Title:** How It All Started

Commerce Wealth was born out of a simple observation — commerce students in Jamshedpur deserved better. While engineering and medical coaching centres thrived, commerce students were often left to navigate a dense syllabus with little structured support.

Vasu Sir and Aditya Sir, both passionate educators with deep expertise in commerce subjects, decided to change that. They started with a small batch, a whiteboard, and a commitment: teach every concept until it clicks.

What began as a handful of students in a single room has grown into Jamshedpur's most trusted commerce coaching destination — but the core philosophy has never changed. Small batches. Honest teaching. Real results.

- Issue and Redemption of Debentures
- Financial Statements of a Company
- Analysis of Financial Statements (Ratio Analysis, Cash Flow Statement)

**Section Title:** Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What boards do you cover?**  
A: We provide coaching for CBSE, ICSE (ISC), and JAK Board (Jharkhand Academic Council) for Class 11th and 12th Commerce.

**Q: What subjects are taught at Commerce Wealth?**  
A: We teach Accountancy, Economics (Microeconomics, Macroeconomics & Indian Economy), and Business Studies.

**Q: What is the batch size?**  
A: We keep our batches small — 15 to 20 students maximum — to ensure personal attention and effective doubt-clearing.

**Q: Do you offer a demo class?**  
A: Yes. We offer a free demo class so students can experience our teaching style before enrolling. Contact us to book one.

**Q: Is the coaching only offline?**  
A: Currently, all classes are conducted offline at our Jamshedpur centre. We believe in-person teaching delivers the best results for commerce subjects.

**Q: When do new batches start?**  
A: New batches typically start in April (for fresh academic sessions) and July (for mid-year joiners). Contact us for the latest batch schedule.

**Q: Do you provide study material?**  
A: Yes. All enrolled students receive printed notes, practice sheets, solved examples, and previous year papers as part of their course.

**Q: How are parents kept informed?**  
A: We share regular progress reports, test scores, and attendance updates with parents. Parent-teacher meetings are also conducted periodically.

**Q: Can I join mid-session?**  
A: Yes, mid-session joining is possible. We conduct a short assessment to understand where the student stands and provide catch-up support.

**Q: What are the fees?**  
A: Fees vary by class and subjects chosen. Please contact us directly for the latest fee structure. We believe in keeping our pricing fair and transparent.
